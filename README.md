# Hi, I'm Hasan Ouzoun! 👋

### 💫 My Journey (From Resilience to Code)
My story is one of perseverance. Due to the war in Syria, I moved to Turkey at the age of 9 and worked full-time until I was 17 to support my life. At that time, I only had a 4th-grade primary school education.

In Belgium, I turned my life around:
- 🇧🇪 **Language:** I mastered Dutch from scratch.
- 🎓 **Education:** I successfully completed my secondary education in **ICT & Administration** through **CVO Focus (Tweede Kans Onderwijs)**—a 2-year journey I am incredibly proud of.
- 🚀 **Present:** I am now a first-year **Programming student at Arteveldehogeschool Gent**.

Despite starting with almost no formal schooling, I proved that dedication can bridge any gap. Now, I apply that same grit to writing clean, efficient code.

### 🏆 Achievements I'm Proud Of
* **Educational Leap:** Going from a 4th-grade level to a Bachelor/Graduate level in Programming in a foreign language.
